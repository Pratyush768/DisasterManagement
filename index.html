Our Services

Temporary Shelter

Providing emporary shelters, food, and water are essential components in emergency response, providing immediate relief to those affected by disasters. Temporary shelters offer a safe haven for displaced individuals, protecting them from the elements and offering a sense of security during uncertain times.

Cloud-Based Department Connect

A digital platform accessible via mobile app and website, leveraging secure cloud technology or real-time coordination among hospitals, police, rescue teams, and local governments. It ensures seamless communication, efficient resource allocation, and synchronized task management during disasters.

House ID System

A unique House ID for each household, linked to property records, family details, and Aadhaar card. This ID streamlines access to critical services (shelter, food, medical aid) and simplifies post-disaster processes, including damage assessment, insurance claims, and compensation..
